How to Use

Select an item from the column on the left, then either Left Click to place or Right Click to remove.
Left‑Click + Drag: Place selected item
Alt + Left‑Click: Erase blocks
Shift + Left‑Click: Fill entire chunk
Ctrl + Left‑Click: Clear entire chunk
Scroll Wheel: Zoom
W A S D: Pan the view
